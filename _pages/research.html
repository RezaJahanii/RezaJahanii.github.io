Decentralized Federated Learning in Mobile Networks

Mobility Assisted Decentralized Federated Learning

  • Investigating the impact of mobility on Decentralized Federated Learning (DFL) systems.
  • Developed a data-driven, mobility-assisted DFL framework outperforming the baseline.
  • Convergence and Theoretical Analysis of DFL systems in mobile networks.
  • Comprehensive analysis of network parameters impact on DFL systems.

Catastrophic Forgetting in DFL under Mobile Networks

  • Theoretically demonstrated the catastrophic forgetting phenomenon under mobility in DFL.
  • Designing a robust DFL framework resolving issues raised from catastrophic forgetting.

Publication: arXiv:2505.18866

Deep Learning Based 2D Direction of Arrival Estimation using L-shaped Arrays

  • Developed a deep learning-based method for Direction of Arrival (DOA) estimation.
  • Estimated elevation and azimuth angles of RF sources via observation matrix in multi-source scenarios.
  • Outperformed state-of-the-art methods in accuracy and robustness.

Publication: ScienceDirect: Signal Processing (2024)
